Garlic Herb Chicken with Roasted Potato & Steamed Greens

Grill the chicken, roast the potato, steam the greens — pack separately. Mix at serving. 3–5 days sorted.

Pack each component into separate containers — chicken in one, potato in another, greens in a third. Combine at serving time so nothing goes soggy.
Method
1
MarinadeMix olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt and pepper in a bowl. Add the chicken breasts and coat well. Marinate for at least 15 minutes — overnight in the fridge is even better.
2
PotatoPreheat oven to 210°C. Cut potato into 2–3cm chunks. Toss in olive oil, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Spread on a lined baking tray in a single layer — don't overcrowd or they'll steam instead of roast. Roast for 25–30 min, turning once halfway, until golden and crispy.
3
ChickenHeat a grill pan or heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Grill chicken breasts 5–6 min each side until char marks form and the internal temp hits 75°C. Rest on a board for 5 minutes — this keeps them juicy. Slice into thick pieces once rested.
4
GreensSteam or blanch broccolini and green beans in boiling water for 3–4 minutes until just tender and still bright green. Drain immediately and spread on a tray to cool quickly — this stops them going mushy in the containers.
5
PackOnce everything is fully cooled, portion each component separately into containers. Divide chicken evenly by weight, do the same for potato and greens. Refrigerate immediately. Reheat chicken and potato in the microwave 2 min, greens 45 sec.
Meal Prep Tips
❄️Keeps 4–5 days in the fridge. Chicken can be frozen but potato and greens are best fresh — freeze chicken only if making more than 5 days worth.
⚖️Weigh sliced chicken total, divide by servings for accurate per-serve weight. Do the same for potato. Greens are low enough cal to eyeball.
💡The chicken is great cold too — slice over the potato and greens straight from the fridge for a quick no-reheat meal.
🔄Swap chicken for lean pork loin steaks or turkey breast steaks — same method, same macros roughly. Change the marinade herbs to keep it interesting across the week.
📊Each serving sits around 580–640 kcal. Pair with a yoghurt bowl or banana at another point in the day to work toward your 2196 kcal target.
